Razorpay is one of India’s leading full-stack financial solutions companies, enabling businesses to accept, process, and disburse payments with ease. Founded in 2014, Razorpay has revolutionized digital transactions by offering a seamless and secure payment experience.

Its platform supports various payment modes including credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets. Beyond payment gateways, Razorpay also provides tools for recurring payments, invoices, subscriptions, and even payroll and capital management through RazorpayX and Razorpay Capital.

The platform is trusted by over 50,00,000 businesses across country, from startups to large enterprises. With a strong focus on innovation, security, and scalability, Razorpay continues to empower Indian businesses to grow in the digital economy. Its easy-to-integrate APIs, modern UI, and reliable infrastructure make it a preferred choice for developers. Razorpay is driving India’s fintech evolution.
